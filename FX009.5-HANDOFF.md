## Summary

FX009.5 stabilized the FX009 real-account system. The app remains free-play with mock credits only. No Solana, crypto, real-money, wagering, or automated scoring work was added.

## Current Auth System

- Signup creates a real account with 10,000 mock credits.
- Passwords are hashed with `crypto.scrypt`.
- Sessions use signed httpOnly `fantasyx_session` cookies.
- Server-side `Session` rows store hashed opaque tokens and expiration.
- Expired sessions are deleted when encountered.
- API routes derive the user from the session and do not trust client `userId`.
